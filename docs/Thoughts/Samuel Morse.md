Samuel Finley Breese Morse (April 27, 1791 â€“ April 2, 1872) was an American inventor and painter. After having established his reputation as a portrait painter, in his middle age Morse contributed to the [invention](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invention "Invention") of a single-wire [telegraph](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electrical_telegraph#Morse_System "Electrical telegraph") system based on European telegraphs. He was a co-developer of [Morse code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morse_code "Morse code") and helped to develop the commercial use of telegraphy.
